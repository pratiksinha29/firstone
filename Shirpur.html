About NMIMS Shirpur

NMIMS Shirpur is located at the banks of river Tapi. It is a residential campus for engineering and medical students.

The campus has seperate hostels, mess, gym, swimmimg pools etc. for girls and boys. Apart from this, the academic

section is very big and spacious. There are different lecture rooms and labs for each subjects and branches. There

is a seperate wing for Engineering and Medicakl students. The central library in the academic wing is very helpful

for the students. All the course related and extra curricular books are available kin the library. Talikin about

hostel facilities, the hostel rooms are very big and spacious, can accomodate 4 students in each room. The toilets

and bathrooms are well maintained by the cleaners. The mess facility is also very good. An extra canteen is also

provided in the academic premises for extra food which is payble. There are cricket grounds, football grounds,

basketball courts, squash courts, badminton courts, tennis courts and volleyball courts present inside the campus
